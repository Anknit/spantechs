CSR Research Foundation (CSRRF) is a non-profit organization standing for knowledge
dissemination and facilitating corporates in fulfilling their Corporate Social Responsibilities.We provide effective information on latest developments in different tools, techniques and applications to bring balance between economic, environmental and social imperatives through researches, seminars, symposiums, lectures, workshops, conferences, etc.

CSRRF is organising a one-day Conference on 22 November 2014 at Le Méridien, Windsor Place, Janpath, New Delhi. Eminent speakers and participants from the Urban Development Ministry, Water Resources, Health, IIT, IIM, IIPA, Consulting Organisations, NGOs, City Developers, IT Sector, Real Estate, Corporates and academia dealing in urban issues will deliberate on the essentials of Smart Cities, their planning and implementations.

We invite you and your esteemed organisation to participate in the conference.
Your invaluable support and contribution will make this event a grand success.
